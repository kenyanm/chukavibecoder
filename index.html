the vibecoder manifesto

/prompt

We treat the model like a pair — we describe intent clearly, iterate fast, and never ship what we don't understand.

/build

Working software beats a perfect plan. We'd rather have an ugly deployed app than a beautiful idea in a notebook.

/share

Every session ends in a demo. We learn out loud, in public, in front of people who'll actually use what we build.

joining the community

Four steps, one WhatsApp link away.

  1. 01

    Fill the form

    Name, email, registration number, course and phone — it takes under a minute.

  2. 02

    Get confirmed

    Your details land straight in our roster. You'll get a confirmation email shortly after.

  3. 03

    Join the group

    We'll add you to the WhatsApp community where sessions, resources and jobs get posted.

  4. 04

    Show up and build

    Come to the next build session with a laptop. Bring an idea, or borrow one of ours.
